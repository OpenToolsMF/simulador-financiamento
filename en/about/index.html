About Installment Map

An independent tool for understanding financing scenarios before comparing real conditions.

← Back to the simulator

Last updated: July 20, 2026.

Installment Map was created to help people visualize installments, outstanding balance, interest, monetary correction, and the effects of extra amortizations in SAC and Price financing.

Project purpose

The tool organizes user-provided data into a clear simulation, with a summary, charts, scenario comparison, and installment schedule. Its purpose is to support study, planning, and approximate checking of scenarios.

Independence

The site does not belong to a bank, banking correspondent, broker, insurer, or financial institution. It also does not intermediate credit, approve operations, or present binding offers.

Data and privacy

Calculations run in the browser itself. The site has no user registration, proprietary backend, or database to receive simulation values. Preferences are saved locally on the device.

Limitations

Results are estimates with no legal, contractual, or financial validity. Real contracts may include specific rules, exact dates, insurance, fees, taxes, variable indexes, and criteria defined by the financial institution.
