This policy explains how Installment Map works, which information remains in your browser, and which technical data may be processed by services required for hosting, usage measurement, and advertising.

Controller and contact

This website is operated by Mapa das Parcelas. For questions, requests, or privacy-related matters, contact us at mapadasparcelas@gmail.com.

Data that stays on your device

The simulation runs in this browser. Values are not sent to Installment Map, and the site has no user registration, backend, or proprietary database that receives them.

  • Simulation values and parameters, such as financing amount, term, rates, dates, amortization system, monetary adjustment, costs, and extra payments, so the last simulation can be restored.
  • Language preference.
  • A record that the short privacy notice was dismissed.
  • Local caches of the TR and Selic reference rates provided by the project static files.

This information remains in local browser storage until it is replaced or deleted. Rate caches have a technical expiration and are renewed when needed.

The simulator “Reset” button deletes saved simulation values and parameters, but keeps the language, notice dismissal, and rate caches.

To remove everything, delete this website data and storage in your browser settings.

Technical data and third-party services

The site is hosted by GitHub Pages. To deliver and protect the page, the hosting service may process technical data such as IP address, visited URL, date and time, browser information, and security logs.

Google Analytics is used to measure use of the site. Its default implementation may collect page visits and events, session statistics, approximate geolocation, browser and device information, and a random identifier in a first-party cookie. GA4 uses the IP address during collection and, according to Google, does not log or store it.

Google AdSense is used to display and measure ads. Depending on your privacy choices and applicable settings, Google and its partners may use or read cookies and receive data such as IP address, visited URL, browser or device identifiers, and ad interactions.

When Google presents a privacy or consent message, use its options to accept, reject, or adjust the use of data for measurement and advertising. You can also control cookies and site data in your browser settings.

See the official information from the services used by this site:

  • Data safeguards in Google Analytics
  • GitHub General Privacy Statement

Purposes and applicable legal bases

  • Make the site available, deliver its content, and maintain service security and integrity.
  • Measure visits, sessions, and page usage to understand and improve the site.
  • Display, limit, personalize when permitted, and measure ads.
  • Keep preferences on the device that are needed to restore the simulation and the selected experience.

Essential technical data may be processed to provide and protect the service and pursue applicable legitimate interests. Cookies, identifiers, measurement, or ad personalization that require consent will be handled according to the choices presented by Google and applicable law.

Sharing and international transfers

Installment Map does not sell or share your simulation values because it does not receive them. Technical data may be processed by GitHub, Google, and partners involved in hosting, security, and advertising.

These providers operate infrastructure in different countries. Technical data may therefore be transferred and processed outside Brazil according to each provider's policies, contracts, and protective measures.

Storage period

Local data remains on the device until you reset the simulation, change preferences, or clear site data. Installment Map does not keep a copy of these values.

Retention periods for hosting logs, cookies, identifiers, analytics, and advertising are defined by GitHub, Google, and their partners in their own policies and settings.

Your privacy rights

Under the Brazilian General Data Protection Law (LGPD), you may request confirmation of processing, access, correction, information about sharing, anonymization, blocking, deletion, portability when applicable, objection, and withdrawal of consent. Because simulation values remain only in your browser, you can delete them directly as described above.

To exercise rights related to processing under this site's responsibility, write to mapadasparcelas@gmail.com.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated to reflect changes to the site, the services used, or the law. The date of the latest version will always appear at the beginning of this page.
